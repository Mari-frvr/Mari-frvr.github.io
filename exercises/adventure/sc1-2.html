The Trip of the Sea

You and your family are on an island vacation and after completing your itinerary, ponder on the next activity to do, as it’s only 5 o’ clock. Out of ideas, you decide to ask the locals. They suggest you go to the old Sailman Billy’s dock since he’s the oldest resident alive and he knows the island like the back of his hand.

But, once you and your family head over you find that Billy’s not there, instead there’s a strange old man by a boat. You ask who he is, turns out he’s Jack, Billie’s brother. He offers to take you on a trip to the sea. What will you do?



Go with Jack, in hopes of admiring the beauty of the
ocean and spotting some of the local sea life.
