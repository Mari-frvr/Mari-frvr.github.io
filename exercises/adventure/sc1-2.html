The Trip of the Sea

Story

The trip starts peacefully, but dark clouds soon gather, and the wind howls through the sails. Jack’s calm fades as towering waves crash against the boat. Lightning flashes, revealing strange shadows beneath the water. He turns back through the storm, fighting the current. When you finally reach the dock, the rain stops—but the beach is deserted. Chairs overturned, belongings scattered—the entire island has been evacuated.
