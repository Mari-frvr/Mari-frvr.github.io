The Trip of the Sea

Story

As Jack sails farther than you expected, the water begins to shimmer in shades of turquoise and gold beneath the setting sun. Suddenly , dolphins leap beside the boat, and Jack smiles knowingly. He tells you to hold on tight as he steers toward a cluster of rocks. Behind them, a secret lagoon reveals itself—hidden from tourists for generations.

The air is warm and peaceful, and bioluminescent fish begin to glow as twilight falls. You realize you’ve just discovered the island’s best-kept secret—a memory your family will treasure forever. Turns out, Jack wasn't so bad afterall.
